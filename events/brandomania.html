The main focus of the event is on enhancing the brand creativity and product innovation proposed by the participating students, through their brand idea, design, and communications. Here the participants are required to come up with a brand of their own, devise strategies and showcase their skills in promoting and marketing the brand. For more details: Click here



Event format:

  • Round 1: QUIZ-O-MANIA


    A quiz competition based on the brands and branding concepts around you. Teams will be shortlisted based on the results.

  • Round 2: AD-O-MANIA


    The shortlisted teams will need to Reimagine an existing product in the allotted categories from the Retail sector by showcasing an advertisement for the same taking current and future market situation into mind.

  • Round 3: BRAND-O-MANIA


    Top 5 Teams will be participating in Final Case study Round where they will be tested on originality, clarity, uniqueness, Innovative approach, Feasibility, sustainability, and scalability for the Brand.


Event rules:

  • The event is open to students from all B-schools across India.
  • The team should be of 2-3 members. Teams can be a mix of 1st and 2nd-year students.
  • All the members of the team should be from the same college.
  • The Quiz will contain 40 questions and the duration will be 10 minutes.
  • Every team will get only one opportunity to take the quiz.
  • For Ad-O-Mania, teams are required to make a "Advertisement" Video not more than 59 seconds along with 1-2 slides mentioning the product description.(More Details on D2C)
  • For Brand-O-Mania, Top 5 Teams would have given a case study on final day and have to come up with presentation within 3 hours.
  • Delay in submission of cases will result in disqualification of teams.
  • Amendments or re-submission are NOT allowed after the files have been sent.
  • The decision of Team Brand-O-Mania 2020 in the matter of eligibility and authenticity shall be final and binding.
