RETAIL MELA

About The Event

Melas are where we congregate to eat, enjoy, be entertained or simply, have a good time.
Have you ever imagined experiencing a mela on a virtual platform? Here is your chance to explore and re-imagine a mela with us! "E-Retail Mela" is an online zone that brings the classic mela feel to a digital platform.
From intense performances aimed at showcasing the best of talent, to live events that celebrate the best of our culture and community, we aim to provide a memorable experience to one and all. And there are plenty of games to enjoy. Regardless of how you score, we're sure it'll score high on your list of unique experiences in 2020. Come be a part of our virtual endeavour and get a chance to win bragging rights and e-certificates.
